Hello! This bot combines the convenience of Telegram, the power of ChatGPT, and the fun of Tinder.

The chatbot can:
Generate a Tinder profile from a description 😎
Write interesting and intriguing messages for starting conversations 😘
Engage in conversations on your behalf 😇🔥
Wractice chatting with the bot 🥰
Generate quotes for your profile using AI

Useful commands and links:

1. /start - main bot menu
2. /profile - generate a Tinder profile 😎
3. /opener - message for starting a conversation 🥰
4. /message - conversation on your behalf 😈
5. /date - conversation with celebrities 🔥
6 /gpt - ask ChatGPT any question 🧠
7. /quote - generate quotes for your profile 🔥
